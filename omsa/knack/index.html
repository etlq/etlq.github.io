Knack Free Tutor Service by Georgia Tech

Georgia Tech offers free tutor service for students on a platform called Knack.
https://grad.gatech.edu/knack-tutoring
It's free to students because the tutors are hired & paid by GT, and they are verified to be students who previously took the course and earned letter grade A.

Unfortunately, not every course is eligible. Among the OMS courses, I've seen CSE 6040, ISYE 6414, ISYE 6740, ISYE 6669 being available on Knack. They really should make more courses available on Knack.

My experience using Knack tutoring service

I've used it many times, and found it super useful. Because the tutors went through (mostly) the same homework and exams in precious semesters, they really know how to best study and prepare for each specific assignment. My tutor definitely helped me score better.

For example, for both 6414 (Regression) and 6669 (Deterministic Optimization), I got the overall 90+% score to barely secure an A right around the cutoff threshold. I probably would've ended up with a B without my tutor's help.
